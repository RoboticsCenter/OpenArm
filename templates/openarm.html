CAN buses

Buses use CAN-FD 1M/5M. Bring-up needs sudo.

Teleoperation

Start with Monitor: both arms stay limp — hand-move a leader joint and check its follower reads track. When the numbers agree, press Start following. Followers begin at their current pose (no jump) and mirror the leader's motion 1:1 over the full physical range. Disconnected joints are skipped automatically. If left and right are crossed, press Swap L/R sides.
