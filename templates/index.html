Calibrate motors

Calibration finds each joint's range of motion and sets its zero point. It's required once before you can control the motors. Keep the area clear — the joints will move on their own. You can hit E-STOP at any time.

Manual calibration —

Press Enable to move, then use the slider to drive the joint all the way to one hardstop and mark it — then the other. When both stops are marked, hit Confirm & Zero and the joint centers itself and sets its zero automatically.
